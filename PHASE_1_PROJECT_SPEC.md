Tagline:

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Pakistan has many government services that ordinary citizens struggle to navigate.

People often don't know

• Which office to visit
• Which documents are required
• Official fees
• Processing time
• Whether an officer is requesting unnecessary documents
• Whether unofficial payments are being requested

Many citizens therefore rely on middlemen.

CivicAI solves this by acting as an AI Civic Decision Assistant.

It is NOT a chatbot.

It is an AI Decision Assistant.

The AI should guide citizens through government procedures using structured government knowledge.

Mock government data is acceptable.
